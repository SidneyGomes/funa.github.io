What are we all about

Brief

Funassyi (ふなっしー, Funasshī) is not just a mascot; it's a symbol of exuberance and community spirit. Its journey began with a simple mission: to bring smiles to the faces of Funabashi residents. As this pear fairy burst onto the scene, it quickly captured the hearts of locals and, soon after, the entire nation.

Cultural Icon

What sets Funassyi apart is its multifaceted presence in Japanese pop culture. From gracing events and festivals to making waves on TV programs and commercials, this whimsical character has seamlessly woven itself into the fabric of Japanese entertainment.

Solana

Funassyi is built on the Solana blockchain, which provides high performance, low fees and scalability to provide the best experience for our users

Unmistakable

While officially remaining an unofficial character, Funassyi's popularity has propelled it to the forefront of official events for Chiba prefecture, the city of Funabashi, and Japan as a whole. Its high name recognition and undeniable appeal make it a go-to symbol for spreading joy and positivity.
